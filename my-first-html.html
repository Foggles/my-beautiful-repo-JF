How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Acquire Two Slices of Bread, Butter, Ham & Cheese.
  2. Butter One Side of each Slice of Bread.
  3. Cut Slice of Cheese.
  4. Place Ham and Cheese onto Buttered Slices of Bread.
  5. Stack Them.
  6. Boom. Sandwich.
